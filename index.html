I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Zürich in the group of Ravit Helled working on the formation, evolution and interiors of giant planets.

Research

My main research interest lies in the formation, evolution and interiors of gas giants in- and outside our solar system. If that sounds interesting, check out the publications I've been a part of.
